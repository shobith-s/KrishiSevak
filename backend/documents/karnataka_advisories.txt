Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) in Karnataka: This is a central government scheme providing income support to all landholding farmer families. Under this scheme, Rs 6,000 per year is provided in three equal installments. The funds are directly transferred to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.

Control of Tomato Fruit Borer (Helicoverpa armigera): This is a major pest for tomato crops in the Mysuru region. For organic control, spray Neem Oil (1500 ppm) at a rate of 5ml per liter of water every 10 days. For chemical control, spraying Coragen (Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC) at 0.3ml per liter of water is highly effective.
